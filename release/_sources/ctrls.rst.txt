Controllers & TSU
=================

This chapter describes the different variants of controllers and TimeStamp Unit (TSU).
The controllers are typically located in the ``comp/ctrls/`` directory in the OFM repository.
The TSU modules are typically located in the ``comp/tsu/`` directory in the OFM repository.
